================================================================================
MODEL DETAILS
================================================================================

ThoughtTrace:
  - reasoning: str (min_length=1, validated)
  - intent_summary: str
  - complexity: Literal["simple", "complex"]
  - timestamp: datetime (auto-set)

Observation:
  - source: str
  - success: bool
  - data: Optional[Dict[str, Any]] (required when success=True)
  - error: Optional[str] (required when success=False)
  - duration_ms: float (>= 0)
  - Validation: model_post_init checks success/data consistency

DecisionEntry:
  - phase: Literal["plan", "think", "act", "observe"]
  - action: str
  - rationale: str
  - timestamp: datetime (auto-set)

DecisionLog:
  - entries: List[DecisionEntry]
  - add_entry(entry: DecisionEntry) -> None

PTAOResult:
  - thought: ThoughtTrace
  - observation: Observation
  - decision_log: DecisionLog
  - raw_result: Dict[str, Any]
  - Supports: model_dump(), model_dump_json()
