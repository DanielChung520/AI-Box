   - Sets up formatters, handlers, loggers

2. structlog.configure(
     logger_factory=structlog.stdlib.LoggerFactory(),

   - Delegates logging to stdlib LoggerFactory
   - Both structlog.get_logger() and logging.getLogger() route through handlers
